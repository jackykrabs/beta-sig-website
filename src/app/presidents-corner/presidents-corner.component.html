President's Corner

Parker Gilliam

Active Chapter President

Spring 2022. What a ride it has been up to this point. I pledged Beta Sig's Iota chapter in the fall of 2020 amidst times of chaos in the world, and doing so provided me with a place to call home. Now, following a semester where we made trips to Kansas City and Chicago, participated in Homecoming, achieved our highest semester GPA since I have been a member, had brothers in the Faurot Field press box and on the rugby field, we move on to the spring semester. We are excited to welcome a new class of Beta Sigs, and encourage anybody interested in becoming a part of the chapter to contact any of our rush chairs. With major chapter house renovations coming just around the corner, Greek Week in just a couple of weeks, and plenty of events to come, there is no better time to be a Beta Sig.
Parker Gilliam
