President's Corner

Parker Gilliam

Active Chapter President

Fall 2022. Two years in college have flown by for me, in large part because of the experiences I've had with Beta Sig. The spring was no exception, as we participated in Greek Week with the men of Delta Chi and women of Zeta Tau Alpha, ventured to South Padre Island for spring break, welcomed new brothers into the chapter, held a wonderful mom's weekend, and so much more. Now, we look towards a fall that is sure to be exciting and busy. With homecoming, football season, dad's weekend, and plenty of other events to come this semester, there is a lot to look forward to at Beta Sig. If you or someone you know is interested in joining our chapter, please reach out to one of our rush chairs or fill out the rush form on this site. As always, Per Aspera Ad Astra.
Parker Gilliam
